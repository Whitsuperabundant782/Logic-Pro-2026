## 📝 Basic Operations 🎼

Logic-Pro-2026 organizes your work into tracks. You can create different types of tracks to build your song:

* Audio Tracks: Use these to record microphones, guitars, or external hardware.
* Software Instrument Tracks: Use these to trigger internal synthesizers and samplers with a MIDI keyboard.
* Drum Tracks: Use these to build beats using built-in percussion kits.

To record, click the "Record Enable" button on your chosen track. Click the red "Record" button in the transport bar to start capturing your performance. The transport bar also holds your Play, Stop, and Rewind controls.

## 💡 Troubleshooting Common Issues 🔧

If you experience audio stuttering, increase your buffer size in the Audio Settings menu. High CPU usage causes stuttering. Close other programs while you work on your music to keep your system responsive.

If the application fails to start, verify your Windows system is up to date. Installing the latest audio drivers from your hardware manufacturer often fixes playback problems. Contact your hardware provider if your audio interface does not appear in the settings menu.
